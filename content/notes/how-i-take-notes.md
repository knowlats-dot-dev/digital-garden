# How I Take Notes

My note-taking process has evolved significantly over the years. Here's how it works today.

## The Workflow

1. **Capture** — I write everything in [[Obsidian Workflow|Obsidian]] using a daily note
2. **Process** — Each evening I turn fleeting notes into permanent atomic notes
3. **Connect** — I always ask "what does this remind me of?" and add links

## Tools I Use

- **Obsidian** — for writing and linking (see [[Obsidian Workflow]])
- **This digital garden** — for publishing and sharing
- The [[Zettelkasten Method]] underpins all of it

## Why It Works

The key insight is that **writing is thinking**. When you have to put something in your own words and link it to what you already know, you genuinely understand it.
