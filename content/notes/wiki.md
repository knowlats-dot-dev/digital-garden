# Wiki

A wiki is a collaboratively edited collection of hyperlinked documents. The term comes from the Hawaiian word _wiki_ meaning "quick."

## Key Characteristics

- **Hyperlinked** — every page can link to any other page
- **Editable** — knowledge evolves over time
- **Non-linear** — no required reading order

## Personal Wiki vs. Public Wiki

This digital garden is a personal wiki — it reflects my thinking rather than a consensus. That's intentional. See [[Welcome to My Digital Garden]] for more on the philosophy.
